Facilities for women

Delhi Metro is fully committed towards safety and security of Women Passengers. Delhi Metro understands the needs of women passengers and has reserved a car especially for lady passengers. The first car of a train in moving direction is reserved for ladies in Yellow, Blue, Green, Violet, Pink & Magenta lines and common earmarked car in both direction movements in Red line. Adequate signages have been provided to clearly indicate the area of platform where the reserved car normally stops.
In addition to this, earmarked seats with adequate signage are reserved in other cars for lady passengers.

Parking and Bicycle facilities

Parking lots have been provided at Metro stations to facilitate commuters to park their two wheeler and/or Four Wheelers at station and use metro service. The parking lots have been outsourced to the contractors who are solely responsible for the safety and security of all the vehicles parked in parking lot and is liable to pay compensation in case of theft of vehicles or damage to vehicles. DMRC supervises smooth operations and adherence to provisions made in contract by the contractors.

Facilities for differently abled passengers

Delhi Metro is perhaps India's first public transport system with adequate features for differently-abled persons. These include:
1. Signage at prominent locations are provided for disabled and mobility restricted passengers.
2. Seats in trains have been reserved for differently abled/senior citizens
3. Train door closing audio/visual indication. Next station and arrival with correct side platform announcements
4. In addition, station staff constantly monitors passenger movement and provides personal help to any differently-abled person who needs assistance
